Hashigo Zake takes the provision of fine beer to new levels.

Seven days a week, 362 days a year1, we serve the best that New Zealand's booming craft brewing industry has to offer.

We are also building the best range of imported beer that New Zealand has seen. We deal directly with breweries in Australia, Japan and the US and import many of the best beers in the Pacific rim. We also have access to some of the best beers coming from Europe.

We open at midday every day.

Our location is 25 Taranaki St, Te Aro, Wellington, New Zealand.
